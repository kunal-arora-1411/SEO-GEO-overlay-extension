# Privacy Policy

**SEO & GEO Optimizer**
**Last Updated: March 2026**

## 1. Introduction

SEO & GEO Optimizer ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension and web service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Display name (optional)
- Encrypted password

### 2.2 Page Analysis Data
When you analyze a web page, our extension extracts:
- Page URL and domain
- Page title and meta description
- Heading structure
- Content text (for analysis only)
- Link counts
- Structured data presence

**Important:** We do NOT collect passwords, form data, cookies, browsing history, or any personal data from the pages you visit beyond what is needed for SEO/GEO analysis.

### 2.3 Usage Data
We collect anonymous usage analytics:
- Features used (analysis, suggestions, exports)
- Extension interaction events
- Error reports

### 2.4 Billing Information
Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We store:
- Stripe customer ID
- Subscription status and tier

We do NOT store credit card numbers or payment details directly.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- **Provide Analysis:** Page content is sent to our servers for AI-powered GEO scoring and rewrite suggestions.
- **Improve Service:** Anonymous usage data helps us improve features and fix bugs.
- **Account Management:** Email is used for authentication and important service communications.
- **Billing:** To manage your subscription and process payments via Stripe.

## 4. Data Retention

- **Analysis Results:** Stored for 90 days, then deleted.
- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
- **Usage Analytics:** Anonymized and retained for up to 12 months.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. We share data only with:
- **OpenAI:** Page content is sent to OpenAI's API for analysis (subject to OpenAI's privacy policy).
- **Stripe:** Payment processing.
- **Sentry:** Error tracking (no personal content data).

## 6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures:
- HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
- Bcrypt password hashing
- JWT token authentication
- Database encryption at rest

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Export your analysis history
- Delete your account and data
- Opt out of analytics

Contact us at privacy@seo-geo-optimizer.com for data requests.

## 8. Chrome Extension Permissions

Our extension requests these permissions:
- **activeTab:** To analyze the current page when you click "Analyze"
- **storage:** To save your preferences and auth token locally
- **scripting:** To inject the analysis overlay on pages

We request **no** permissions for browsing history, bookmarks, or other browser data.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be communicated via the extension or email.

## 10. Contact

For privacy questions: privacy@seo-geo-optimizer.com
